Pictures

A couple of pictures from Angkor Wat in Cambodia

Angkor

I love photography, and I love being able to enhance an image so that it represents what I experienced when I took the shoot. My photos from Angkor in Cambodia is a perfect example of such an ocation.


Defenders of the "unspoiled" and untouched image, the images without any form of editing, would argue that all pictures should be left untouched. They would say that the original image, and that image alone, represents what's real and true. I couldn't disagree more. Just as James Nachtwey says, I don't believe in an objective reality that goes for everyone, I believe in the personal reality that we - you and me - experience on our own. The images below are highly edited, but they reflect my reality.
